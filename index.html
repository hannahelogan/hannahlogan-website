Welcome to my website! My name is Hannah Logan, and I created this site as part of my LIB 841 course at Eastern Kentucky University.

I’m currently working toward my master’s degree in Library Science with the goal of becoming a high school librarian.

This fall, I’m excited to begin a new chapter as a 9th and 10th grade English teacher at Corbin High School.

Through this site, I look forward to sharing my ideas, experiences, and resources as I grow in both the classroom and the library world.

FUN FACT: I am from the small town of Manchester, Kentucky!
